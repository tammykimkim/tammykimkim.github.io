About

Melissa T

I've had a love for photography ever since I can remember...

..."Capturing moments is my passion.
That's what photography is all about - capturing a moment and being able to keep it forever. It reminds you of the good times, togetherness, how much the kids have grown and even how "different" you looked "back then".

I enjoy working with families as I love kids. I actually have a small family myself. My own parents are in Toronto and my two daughters and I live here in Mississauga. I have been told that I have a great sense of humour and am extremely patient and laid back. I also enjoy nature photography and just plain 'being' in the outdoors!"

- Melissa T.

Photography

"Photography to me is catching a moment which is passing, and which is true."
- Jacques-Henri Lartigue

“A good snapshot keeps a moment from running away." ― Eudora Welty

I charge $200 flat rate. Sessions are around 1-2 hours.

Location of your choice. Usually outdoors. Or indoors if I'm capturing a celebration. I give you ALL the photos on USB. Average number of shots: 180-200.

I will also prepare any or all photos in black and white in addition to the colour shots.
